About us

We are the Crystallis Holdings Administration, an agency run by the United Nations of Earth.

The Crystallis was found for the first time with a NASA telescope in 2017, however, once greater technology was invented, the United Earth Space Administration (daughter of the United Nations,) further analyzed the system in 2141, finding life on Crystallis. The Crystallis Holdings Administation and the Tourism Department runs at a loss, with us being funded exclusively by taxes.

In 2143, the Crystallis Planet Charter was written up, and the Crystallis Holdings Administration was founded shortly thereafter. We have been running this planet for over 50 years, and we are very well-versed in the intracacies of Crystallis,

This website is specifically run by the Tourism Department of the CHA, after the treaty of Manama permitted government-sanction tourism. However, we are not the only agency here. Private lodging companies run hotels here, the United Nations Science Administation runs experiments here, and the other departments of the CHA run their operations here.
