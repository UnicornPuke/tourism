Fertilis quattor: "Goblet Plant"

(No images can legally be recorded on the surface of Crystallis for non-scientific purposes, so only sketches may be shown.)

Fertilis quattor, also known as the "Goblet Plant," named after the fact it grips its fruits like a person holding a goblet is a fruit-bearing plant native to the Green River basin in south Lurætia, and is invasive in Ëurand. The seeds in the fruit are not noticable by creatures eating them. The seeds (in a non-invasive way) implant themselves into the lining of the digestive system, to be planted once the creatures die, a behavior common for plants on Crystallis. Over time, they have evolved to make their fruits as appealing and delicious as possible. They are also one of the few plants that contain almost nothing that is poisonous to anything around it. The plant's aim is not to stay alive, but rather to get its fruits eaten. The fruits are relatively tiny, and are intended to be eaten by smaller animals. It also has sun catchers growing out of the ground, equivalent to Earth's leaves. It also has red "veins," that carry nutrients around the plant. Like all plants and photosynthesisers on Crystallis, it uses the purple retinal for photosynthesis, thought to be used on Earth before chlorophyll.
