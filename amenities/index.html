Amenities

Some of the amenities you can expect during your time on Crystallis are immensely accomodating, luxurious, and comfortable.

Our galaxy famous "yellows," a colloquial name for the hotels, since most, if not all, of the hotels on the planet have yellow-based interior decorations. We have a large range of yellows, most of which are privately owned, and you need to book. If you see a company that is running hotels and is not on the approved list of hotel companies, it is illegal and should be reported.

Approved hotel companies:
- Hilton
- Yellow Hotel company
- Entry Hospitality
- Crystallis Hotels (This one is owned by us.)

Don't worry about being disonnected from your relatives or events on Earth, because of GalactiNet, Crystallis has some of the best internet in the galaxy, with internet on the planet being completely public and free.

We have a top-notch network of staffers on Crystallis, from our amazingly trained tour guides, to our friendly police, keeping criminals in check, to our scientists, who are very nice and friendly towards tourists, and are very patient if you ask them questions. Currently, the Tourism Department of the Crystallis Holdings Administration is not hiring, however, some of the other branches might be, so check their pages for more information.

Its star, Jargon has a dyson swarm flying around the planet, a network of high-caliber solar panels, like the one around the Earth providing it with energy. According the Crystallis Planet Charter, no polluting or mining-related activities can be conducted on Crystallis, so you can enjoy your visit with nice, clean, guilt-free air.
